Hamilton Heights

Why It's the Perfect Place to Live

Historic Charm

Live surrounded by the rich legacy of Alexander Hamilton. The neighborhood combines quiet, historic brownstone streets with an energetic, welcoming community vibe.

Green Space Next Door

Steps away from 139th Street and Amsterdam is St. Nicholas Park. It runs right alongside the neighborhood, giving you beautiful hills, trees, and an instant outdoor escape from the city pavement. St. Nicholas Park also has the largest dog park in the city!

A Commuter's Dream

The local sidewalk paths lead directly to the train platforms. Catching the downtown train makes getting to Midtown, Times Square, or Hunter College easy and affordable.

History

Home of the historic Hamilton Grange mansion.

Nature

Plentiful green acres at St. Nicholas Park.

Transit

Direct subway access right down the block.
