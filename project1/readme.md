# My Time in Cambridge
From 2015 to 2018, I lived in [Cambridge](https://www.britannica.com/place/Cambridge-England) with my family. My mom was attending the [University of Cambridge](https://www.cam.ac.uk) to study English. In August, 2018, my family moved from our home in [Lancaster, Pennsylvania](https://www.cityoflancasterpa.gov) to Cambridge. We stayed with my grandfather in his house in [Llangynidr, Wales](https://www.mon-and-brec-canal.wales/llangynidr/), for a few weeks. Then we drove to our new house on ![Histon Road](histonroad.jpg).
