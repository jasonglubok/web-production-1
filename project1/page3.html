Llangynidr, Wales

Before we officially moved to Cambridge, we stayed with my grandfather for a few weeks. My grandfather lives in a small town in Wales called Llangynidr.

During our stay, we visited the seaside town of Tenby with my grandfather and explored several historic Welsh castles. One of the most famous castles in the region is Caerphilly Castle.

It is a fun fact about Tenby that the future King Henry VII (dad of Henry VIII) escaped through a tunnel in Tenby when people were trying to capture him.
